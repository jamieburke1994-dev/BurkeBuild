Our specialism - Part M works

Accessibility,
without compromise.

Part M of the Building Regulations exists so that homes work for everyone. We specialise in accessible adaptations that are safe, compliant and genuinely pleasant to live with - never clinical, never an afterthought.

  • Level-access ramps, thresholds & handrails
  • Accessible wet rooms & bathroom adaptations
  • Widened doorways, circulation & stairlift prep
  • Works supported under adaptation grant schemes
  • Compliant, certified & finished to a high standard
