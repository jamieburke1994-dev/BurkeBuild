Contemporary Rear Extension

A single-storey, flat-roof extension across the full rear of a two-storey home - opening the ground floor onto a new slate patio through full-width glazing.

The brief

Open up a closed-off ground floor, bring in light from the garden side and give the family a real indoor-outdoor connection - without losing the character of the existing house above.

What we did

  • Single-storey flat-roof extension across the full rear elevation
  • Full-width glazed sliding doors and floor-to-ceiling picture windows
  • Glazed canopy panels carrying light deep over the patio threshold
  • Wrap-around fascia with concealed rainwater goods
  • Natural slate patio laid to falls with discreet drainage
  • Rendered finish and external lighting to tie old and new together

The result

The back of the house now runs as one continuous space - kitchen and living areas flowing straight out onto the slate terrace. On a wet Irish evening, the glazing does the work; on a dry one, the patio doubles the living space.
